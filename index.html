Features

Simple Pomodoro Timer

No complicated menus, animations or fluff. Set session length, then focus till the alarm goes off. Quartz automatically switches the timer to it's next mode.

White Noise Player

Listen to the soothing sounds of the wind and ocean, mixed with authentic white noise, to help lower stress and block out distractions.

Runs from Lock Screen

Without draining your battery.

Designed in California

Quartz is a mobile app based on the "Pomodoro Technique", a time-managment solution developed by Francesco Cirillo in the late 1980's. We combined audio crafted specifically to reduce background noise with a minimalist design, to increase productivity while simultaneously lowering stress!
